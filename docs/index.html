Procedural Compliance Framework

Templates GitHub

We the People establish this framework to document the procedure by which rights become remedy. Courts shall provide written findings. Clerks shall maintain faithful records. Counsel shall fulfill statutory duty. When procedure fails, liberty fails. We publish openly that all may enforce the law's promise.
